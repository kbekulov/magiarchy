Russia invaded Ukraine
Russia invaded Ukraine on February 24th, 2022. This invasion was announced by Vladimir Putin in a late-night video address to the nation, and ~200,000 soldiers of the Russian Armed Forces entered Ukrainian territory from the south, east, and north.
